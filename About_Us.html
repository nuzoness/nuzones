About us

We turn every ride into an adventure. Combining navigation, competition, and rewards.

Our mission

At NuZones, our mission is to make biking adventures more accessible, more affordable, and more rewarding.

We believe the best rides aren't locked behind expensive events — they’re waiting to be explored, anytime you want. NuZones brings a fresh challenge to familiar trail networks, giving regular riders a new reason to return, and offering first-time visitors a smart guide through the best routes.

Every time you ride with NuZones, you’re also giving back. We donate a portion of each ride to support local trail associations, helping maintain and grow the trails we all love.

Can't make the dig day?

We get it, life’s busy. But every ride with NuZones still gives back to the trails.

By riding, you're helping fund maintenance, new lines, and stronger trail networks for the future, no shovel required.

Ride hard. Support harder.
