Alex's Story

NuZones started with a simple idea, riding should be about adventure.

The Starting Point

I moved up to Scotland for a full-time engineering role with every intention of exploring brand new trails each weekend. However, life got in the way as it does, and I regularly ended up lapping the same old tracks.

To spice things up I would go in search of a something unique and sometimes stumble across gold. This process of adventuring soon became my favourite past time outside of work.

I thought – what if there was a way to share my love of exploring with others.

The Community

From families looking for a weekend outing all the way up to racers wanting a new challenge, NuZones is for anyone who loves exploring trails in a new way.

I’ve always wanted the community to be built around support, inclusivity, and the spirit of exploration. Whether you’re revisiting familiar trails with a fresh purpose, or exploring a network you’ve never ridden before, NuZones gives you a reason to go further and enjoy the adventure.

Supporting the places we ride is just as important. Every ride through NuZones contributes to local trail associations, helping ensure that the trails we love stay open, maintained, and sustainable for future riders.

The Vision

My vision for NuZones is simple. I want it to be available across every major trail centre and network across the UK. Offering new challenges, more reasons to explore, and continuous support to our trails.

While the platform could one day expand beyond cycling our focus right now is firmly on mountain biking and gravel riding.

The aim is for every rider, no matter their experience level to walk away thinking -

"That was awesome!"
