Putting the adventure back into riding

We challenge you to locate tags hidden around a trail network. The tags are placed strategically to encourage you to explore new routes, or to act as a guide if you are new to the area.

Compete for spots on the leaderboard to earn badges, discounts, and prizes.

easy

Navigation

Using our custom built mobile application find ten hidden tags around the trail network.

These tags are QR codes and their physical location matches the digital coordinates found within the app.

The app can operate offline allowing you to explore new areas even without mobile service.

friendly

Competition

Whether you are looking to speed from tag to tag or leisurely explore, each adventure is timed from the first to the last scan.

You have the option to compete solo or in a team (up to four) to become top of your respective leaderboard.

Times are recorded and displayed on your profile which are visible to the community to compare stats.

regular

Rewards

As you progress through the app you can earn achievement badges. These are in-app awards to collect as you complete more adventures with us.

First place riders across all zones and categories are entered into regular raffles to win prizes supplied from our partner companies.
